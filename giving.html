At HMCC, we believe that when God gives someone a vision, He will always provide for that vision. God invites us to partner with Him in His ministry of making disciples and transforming the world. Our financial giving—whether it is regular tithes or additional gifts—is not only an expression of thankfulness and worship unto God, but also an act of faith that God will provide and equip His church for His mission. FAQs

Are there any transaction fees?

An additional 3% of the contribution helps to cover online transaction fees. If you would like to give by mail and avoid the fee, our mailing address is:

HMCC
P.O. Box 4622
Ann Arbor, MI 48106



How is personal information used?

Personal information is used for tax receipt purposes only and will only be seen by the Stewardship Team. If you have any questions, please email stewardship@hmcc.net



Is my donation tax deductible?

HMCC is a IRS-registered 501(c)3 not-for-profit organization.
For online giving, you can use your emailed receipt for tax purposes. For cash and check donations, we'll issue out giving reports at the end of the year.
