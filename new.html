THE GOSPEL

Transformation.

You will see it as you look at the people around you. You will hear about it as you listen to the stories of lives touched by God. At Harvest Mission Community Church, we hope that you will experience God for yourself as you worship with us and get connected to our community of faith. No matter what your stage of life, cultural background, field of study or profession, and no matter where you are in your spiritual journey, we believe that you can experience the transforming power of God at HMCC. We are glad to meet you, and we hope that as you spend time with us you will catch a glimpse of God's desire to transform your life so that you can go out and transform the world.
